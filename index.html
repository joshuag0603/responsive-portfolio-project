About Me

I am a digital designer who focuses on User Experience and User Interface with the goal of increasing accessibility for all types of users. I have primarily been using Figma to create website and app prototypes that allow users to easily understand and navigate the information in front of them. In the past I have worked on projects related to social justice, agriculture, brand marketing, collective action. I am currently interested in pursing projects related to social justice, mental health wellness, and generally increasing the accessibility of websites for all users.
